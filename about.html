About Me

Jourdan Dekle

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Jourdan Dekle and I am a proud graduate of Kennesaw State University. Currently, I attend the University of Florida as I work to obtain my M.A. in Communication and Digital Strategy. I am a member of Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority Inc. and I enjoy vacationing with my family and spending time with my dogs.
